Our baking and pastry courses


We provide classes and resources that will help you make beautiful, delicious desserts that everyone can enjoy. So why not join us and explore the world of baking with Julie's Bakery.

Sourdough bread

Sourdough bread made from wild yeast has been around for thousands of years, and still continues to be popular today. If you're interested in making your own sourdough bread, then taking a course can help you master the art of making it at home.

Date: 20 april 17:00-19:00
Price: 400 kr

Pastry

We'll focus on techniques in making flaky pie crusts, biscuits, croissants, fillings, toppings, and decorating with glazes and icings. Whether you're an experienced baker or just getting started with dessert-making, this course is sure to be a delicious learning experience.

Date: 5 may 17:00-19:00
Price: 400 kr

Learn to decorate

You will learn the basics of cupcake decoration, including how to handle a pastry bag, choose the right tips, and use different types of icing. You will also learn different piping techniques, such as swirls, rosettes, stars, and flowers.

Date: 20 may 13:00-15:00
Price: 400 kr
